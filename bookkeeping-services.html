Our Specialty

Bookkeeping Clean Up

We get it — sometimes life can be messy. It's not uncommon for business owners to find that, despite best efforts, their bookkeeping has descended into disarray. Small business owners often set out to keep their own books in QuickBooks or Quicken, but then fall behind or realize they haven't been doing it quite right. Alternatively, sometimes hired bookkeepers move on from companies, leaving unfinished accounting that's difficult to decipher. Untangling bookkeeping messes requires a specific skillset most people do not possess.

Not to worry; Bookkeeping Strategies LLC has you covered!

We specialize in cleaning up messes, untangling numerical knots, fixing problems and, most importantly of all, providing you with fiscal peace of mind. We work in just about any commonly used accounting software but most of our clients prefer QuickBooks Desktop, QB Online, and industry-specific software.

We will discuss and review your financial systems weekly, monthly, or as often as needed and advise you on best practices so you can focus on what you do best.

Accounting "Fixer"

Paying high CPA rates to fix accounting issues is like hiring a lawyer to do paralegal work. Bookkeeping Strategies LLC will evaluate your books to find and fix any issues — whether on a one-time or a recurring basis. We can straighten out your current system, or set you up with a new system that will grow with your business. We can even provide support and training for you and your staff so you can run at your most efficient level. All our services adhere to the highest standards of professionalism and confidentiality.
