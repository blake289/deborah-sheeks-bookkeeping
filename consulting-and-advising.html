Consulting & Advising Services

You're very good at what you do. But the ins and outs of business finances — that can be tricky. Any veteran business owner can tell you that a good bookkeeping & accounting advisor is crucial to starting a business and keeping it sustainable. Calling Bookkeeping Strategies LLC is the first thing you'll want to do when starting a business, or if you've grown past 'bootstrapping' it on your own. We work with a variety of businesses, providing insightful and timely guidance and advice.

Our unique approach goes beyond traditional bookkeeping services with a customized care to fit your business needs. Some of the services we offer include:

Consulting Services

We tailor our services to your needs, whether new business setup, clean up of existing systems or ongoing support and training for your staff.

Trouble-Shooting Consultation

Evaluation of bookkeeping issues whether it is one time or recurring.

Complete Business Set Up

We evaluate where you are now and where you want to be later. We create a system that grows with your business.

Inventory Setup & Clean-Up

If you are using inventory tracking or would like to expand to use it, we will assess the current state, implement, clean-up and train you in how to do this.

Time Tracking

Do you want to invoice your customers for billable time and expenses? Track time for cost accounting or payroll. We can assist you with implementing this.
